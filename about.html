The problem we're solving

There are over 5 million small businesses in the UK. Most of them are run by brilliant, hardworking people — but very few of them have a finance background. When they need a cash flow forecast, a break-even calculator, or a pricing model, they face an impossible choice.

They can hire a consultant (expensive — hundreds of pounds per hour). They can try to build it in Excel (takes hours, breaks constantly, looks terrible). Or they can use generic online calculators that don't fit their actual business.

None of those options are good enough. Every UK business deserves professional financial tooling — not just the ones that can afford a Finance Director.

That's the gap SpeakeEasy fills. Describe what you need in plain English, and get a professional, working financial tool in seconds — built with real financial expertise baked in, not just generic code.

Why we built it with AI

We could have built a library of pre-made templates. But templates are rigid — they never quite fit your actual situation. Your business is unique, your numbers are unique, and your financial tools should be too.

AI lets us generate tools that are genuinely tailored to what you describe. Ask for a cash flow forecast for a seasonal business and you get seasonal forecasting built in. Ask for a VAT-aware pricing calculator and you get UK VAT logic. The tool fits your business — not the other way around.

And because it's built by someone who actually understands finance, the AI is trained to generate tools that make financial sense — not just tools that look like they work.
