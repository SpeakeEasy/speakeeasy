Frequently asked questions

Can I try before I buy? +
Yes — the Free plan lets you build 3 tools per month with no credit card required. You can upgrade to Pro whenever you're ready.
What does "Excel export with formulas" mean? +
Pro users can download their generated tools as proper .xlsx Excel files with all formulas pre-built. Open in Excel or Google Sheets and the calculations work immediately — no setup needed.
What tool types can I generate? +
All plans support every tool type — budget planners, break-even calculators, cash flow forecasts, ROI trackers, invoice generators, runway calculators, pricing tools, and much more. If it involves numbers, SpeakeEasy can build it.
Can I cancel at any time? +
Absolutely. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What's the white-label option on Agency? +
Agency plan users can brand generated tools with their own logo, colours and company name. Perfect for accountants or advisors who want to offer branded financial tools to their clients.
Do I keep the tools I've already built if I downgrade? +
Yes — any tools you've already downloaded are yours to keep forever. Downgrading only affects how many new tools you can generate going forward.
